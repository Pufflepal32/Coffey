Meet Shawn Coffey

I'm Shawn Coffey, owner of Coffey's Roofing LLC. For over 15 years, I've been helping homeowners across West Virginia and Virginia protect their homes with quality roofing and seamless gutter services.

When I started this company, I had one goal: to provide honest, quality work without the hassle that often comes with hiring a contractor. I've seen too many homeowners get taken advantage of by companies that overpromise and underdeliver. That's not how I do business.

My promise is simple: no hassle at all. You'll get a straight answer, a fair price, and work that's done right the first time. I'm here to make sure all my customers are 100% satisfied — and I mean that.

As a local contractor, my reputation is everything. Every job I complete is a reflection of my commitment to this community. When you call Coffey's Roofing, you're not just getting a contractor — you're getting a neighbor who cares about your home as much as you do.
